Reimagining the web

The web is simultaneously one of humanity's greatest inventions and a mess overdue for an overhaul. People spend a significant portion of their lives online doing soul-sucking tasks, ultimately capped by their bandwidth.

Whether it's simple tasks like ordering groceries or tracking campground reservations, or more complex scenarios like planning and coordinating a group trip — both the floor and the ceiling of human productivity on the web needs to be uplifted.

We are building AI agents that can reliably do everyday digital tasks for you on the web, towards an AI chief-of-staff for everyone. Our mission is to build the best AI assistants to make space for the meaningful things and create yutori in your life.

World-class team

We are building the entire stack to be agent-first, from training our own models to generative product interfaces. We're doing this with a world-class founding team that previously worked at:
